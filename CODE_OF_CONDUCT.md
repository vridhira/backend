# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Assume good intent, but focus on facts and outcomes.
3. Provide actionable technical feedback.
4. Keep communication professional in issues, PRs, and discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
2. Personal attacks, threats, or intimidation.
3. Publishing private or sensitive information without consent.
4. Repeated disruptive behavior after warnings.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking fair corrective action.

Possible actions include:

1. Warning.
2. Temporary restriction from discussions/PRs.
3. Permanent ban from participation.
