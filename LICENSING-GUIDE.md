No implicit commercial rights are granted.

Commercial use begins only after all of the following are complete:

1. Scope review by the Licensor.
2. Execution of COMMERCIAL-LICENSE-AGREEMENT.md.
3. Approval of payment and term under PAYMENT-SCHEDULE.md.

## Common Questions

### "Can I test this inside a company before signing?"

Only for isolated, non-production, non-revenue evaluation environments.
Any production, pre-production with customer traffic, or revenue-facing use
requires prior written approval and a signed agreement.

### "Can I run this as SaaS or managed hosting?"

Not by default. Network or hosted delivery is prohibited unless explicitly
authorized in writing.

### "Can I white-label or resell it?"

Not by default. Any transfer, resale, sublicensing, or white-label scenario
requires explicit written permission and must be named in your approved scope.

### "We are pre-revenue. Do we still need permission?"

Yes, if deployment is for commercial intent or corporate operations.

### "We changed a lot of code. Does this still apply?"

Yes. Derivative works remain subject to the licensing framework.
